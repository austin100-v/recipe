French Fries

Description

These homemade French fries are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside, perfect as a side dish or snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: paprika or garlic powder for seasoning

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips.
  2. Soak the potato strips in cold water for at least 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. Drain and pat the potatoes dry with a clean towel.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry the potato strips in batches until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes per batch.
  6. Remove fries from the oil and drain on paper towels. Season with salt while still hot.
  7. If desired, sprinkle with paprika or garlic powder for extra flavor.

Enjoy your crispy homemade French fries!
